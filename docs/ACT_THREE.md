# Act III — Crown Beneath Snow

Act III begins after Censor Wei falls and the Jade Court archive exposes two forbidden facts: Shi-An was born beneath the Green Dragon imperial constellation, and Prince Jian—the emperor’s missing heir—survived the purge that killed Master Shen.

## Campaign route

1. **Northern Star Pass** — Scout Lan explains that Regent Han is assembling a northern invasion beneath a forged decree.
2. **Iron Prayer Monastery** — Shi-An faces Abbot Stone Vein and wins the monastery standard without abandoning mercy.
3. **Snow Lotus Village** — White Yak General’s siege threatens the winter medicine terraces and the families sheltering there.
4. **Red Banner War Camp** — Captain Ruo reveals that the invasion order carries the regent’s seal rather than the emperor’s.
5. **Sanctum of the First Mask** — Moon Veil leads Shi-An to Father No-Face, founder of the order that once protected hidden children before becoming an assassin network.
6. **Celestial Archive** — the sealed star tables confirm Shi-An’s constellation and Prince Jian’s identity.
7. **Refuge of the Vanished Heir** — Prince Jian agrees to return only after monastery, village and loyal guard stand together.
8. **Black Dragon Observatory** — Shi-An completes the constellation trial and stops Regent Han’s war order.

## Side stories

- **The Bell for the Border Widows** recovers the memorial bell stolen from families mourning the border dead.
- **Medicine Beneath the Ice** protects Snow Lotus Village’s winter medicine stores.
- **The Last Loyal Standard** recovers the final banner of the emperor’s lost guard.

These deeds increase heir trust and make the political resolution feel earned rather than purely hereditary.

## Northern standards

Act III records three standards:

- Iron Prayer Monastery
- Snow Lotus Village
- Loyal Imperial Guard

Each standard grants a small combat bonus in subsequent duels. The Northern War Council, opened with **Z**, shows standards, heir trust, northern renown, the faction war balance and the status of the celestial star map.

## Save compatibility

`actThree` is added through safe enrichment and does not replace the established save format. Existing saves that completed Act II unlock the northern campaign automatically. Act III quest state, standards, war balance, heir trust, northern renown and the star map are persistent.

## Validation

`tests/act-three.mjs` validates static wiring and plays the critical campaign route from unlock through the Black Dragon Observatory finale. The GitHub Pages workflow keeps all Act I, Act II, living-world and original smoke gates in place before deployment.
