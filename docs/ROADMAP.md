# Production roadmap

The current commit is Milestone 1: a playable, asset-complete vertical slice that proves exploration, dialogue, quests, recruitment, trading, training, saving and arcade combat in the browser.

## Milestone 2 — Combat depth

- Three-round duel rules and optional three-fighter exhibition format.
- High/low guard, throws, knockdowns, wall distance and counter-hit windows.
- Bespoke enemy bodies and animation sets for large, agile, elderly and weapon specialists.
- Staff, jian, dao, spear and chain-whip mastery trees.
- Tournament ladder, sparring mode and combat accessibility settings.

## Milestone 3 — Living world

- Tile-based multi-screen maps with interiors, doors, elevation and weather.
- Day/night schedules, inns, ferries, escort caravans and wandering encounters.
- Physically place the fighter and location archives throughout the world.
- Faction reputation, crime/witness logic, rumours and regional prices.
- Party formation, camp conversations and loyalty missions.

## Milestone 4 — Full campaign

- Six acts targeting 6–8 hours for the critical path and 15+ hours with side quests.
- 60+ authored quests, 25 recruitable or temporary allies and multiple endings.
- Shaolin, Wu Dang, Emei, Beggar Kings, escort houses, merchant guilds and outlaw schools.
- Tournament of Ten Thousand Hands and final imperial-court chapters.

## Milestone 5 — Release polish

- Full gamepad remapping and mobile layout refinement.
- Save slots, cloud-save adapter and export/import saves.
- Performance profiling, automated browser tests and localization framework.
- Expanded soundtrack, cinematics, achievements and storefront materials.
